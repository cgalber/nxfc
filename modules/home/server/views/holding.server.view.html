Hi Everyone,

We (Minnie, Ben, Carlos & Oli) are the steering committee of Eat A Rainbow & New Cross Food Coop.

At present, Eat A Rainbow runs a twice weekly fruit and veg stall in New Cross, and New Cross Food Coop runs a co-buying order with Suma (using spreadsheets etc). The food co-op is undergoing a period of development and expansion, and the next thing on our agenda is to get tech working for us. We held a hackday and are frantically building a website to allow us to expand our ordering facilities. You can take a look at our progress on github here

We need you... We need technical people of all flavours, we need webby design people, we need organiser type people, we need people who can author interesting copy, we need people with knowledge of current issues around food, and with interesting ideas about food and how the web can help. We need everyone! If you're interested drop us an email: info AT newcrossfoodcoop.org.uk

Ben, Minnie, Oli & Carlos
